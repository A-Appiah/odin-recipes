Steak and Mashed Potatoes


Once you try this juicy, tender steak recipe you'll start wondering why you haven't seasoned your steak with mustard before.
Ingredients:
  • 800g washed potatoes, peeled, chopped
  • 2 tbsp light sour cream
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • 420g can corn kernels, drained
  • 4 Beef Scotch Fillet Steaks
  • 1/4 cup wholegrain mustard
  • 2 bunches asparagus, woody ends trimmed, steamed
  • 2 tbsp chopped chives
