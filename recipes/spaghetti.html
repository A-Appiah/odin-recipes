Easy Weeknight Spaghetti

Spaghetti is one of the most popular dishes in the world. It is a popular choice for breakfast, lunch, and dinner. It is also one of the most popular foods in the world.

Ingredients:


  • 1 cup spaghetti
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 1 tbsp pepper
  • 1 cup water

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Cook noodles in boiling water until noodles are tender.
  3. Stir diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, and tomato paste into the pan. Season with oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer spaghetti sauce for 1 hour, stirring occasionally.
  4. Mix pasta noodles into sauce.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!
